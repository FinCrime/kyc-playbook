KYC Playbook is live — the world's most comprehensive **free, open-source** learning platform for AML, KYC, EDD, and financial crime compliance.

<!-- truncate -->

## Why I Built This

After years working in AML/KYC compliance — from KYC analyst roles through EDD investigations and multi-jurisdiction SAR filing — I noticed a consistent gap: **high-quality, practical, free educational resources simply didn't exist at scale**.

Most compliance training is:
- Hidden behind expensive certification courses
- Generic and theory-heavy without practical application
- Fragmented across dozens of separate sources
- Not written by people who actually do the work day-to-day

KYC Playbook changes that.

## What's Available Right Now

From day one, the platform includes:

- **50+ full learning pages** covering AML fundamentals, KYC/CDD/EDD, sanctions, PEP screening, OSINT, transaction monitoring, SAR filing, and more
- **7 practice labs** with real-world investigation scenarios, hints, and model solutions
- **500+ interview Q&As** across AML Analyst, KYC Analyst, EDD Analyst, and Compliance QA roles
- **Interactive quizzes** for AML basics, KYC fundamentals, and sanctions screening
- **Cheat sheets and templates** for daily reference
- **CAMS study guide** with an 84-day preparation plan

## Built for the Whole Community

Whether you're:
- A **student** entering compliance for the first time
- A **career-switcher** transitioning into AML/KYC
- A **working analyst** sharpening skills or preparing for promotion
- A **CAMS candidate** studying for certification

There's a structured learning path for you.

## Contribute

KYC Playbook is open-source and community-driven. If you're a compliance practitioner with expertise to share, please contribute. The [Contribution Guide](/docs/introduction/contributing) explains how.

Together, we can build the resource that the compliance community actually deserves.
