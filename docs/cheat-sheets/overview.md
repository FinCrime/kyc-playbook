## How to Use Cheat Sheets

Cheat sheets are not substitutes for the full learning content — they are reference summaries for practitioners who have already studied the underlying material. Use them for:

- Quick recall during active investigations
- Interview preparation (rapid review of key frameworks)
- Daily desktop reference
- Team training aids
