# Transaction Risk

## Overview

Transaction risk assesses the ML/TF risk inherent in the pattern, size, frequency, and counterparties of a customer's actual transaction activity — distinct from their static profile risk.

## Key Transaction Risk Indicators

- **Volume inconsistency** — Transaction volumes inconsistent with declared income/business size
- **Velocity** — Unusually rapid movement of funds in/out of an account
- **Counterparty risk** — Transactions with high-risk jurisdictions or sanctioned entities
- **Structuring patterns** — Multiple transactions just below reporting thresholds
- **Round-dollar amounts** — Unusual frequency of round numbers
- **Dormancy-to-activity** — Sudden activity after a long dormant period
- **Pass-through behavior** — Funds received and immediately transferred out
