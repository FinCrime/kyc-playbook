# Customer Risk

## Overview

Customer risk assesses the inherent ML/TF risk presented by the customer's profile, independent of geography, product, or transaction behavior.

## Key Customer Risk Factors

| Factor | Risk Indicators |
|---|---|
| **Customer type** | Individual (lower) vs. complex entity/trust (higher) |
| **PEP status** | PEPs and RCAs carry elevated corruption-related risk |
| **Occupation/business** | Cash-intensive or high-risk professions elevate risk |
| **Source of wealth complexity** | Easily explained vs. opaque/unverifiable |
| **Adverse media** | Any negative news findings |
| **Relationship history** | New vs. long-standing customer with consistent behavior |
| **Transparency** | Cooperative vs. evasive in providing information |

## High-Risk Customer Categories

- Politically Exposed Persons (PEPs) and their RCAs
- Non-resident customers with no clear nexus to the institution's jurisdiction
- Customers with complex/opaque ownership or trust structures
- Customers in cash-intensive professions (e.g., precious metals dealers)
- Customers previously subject to SARs or law enforcement inquiry
- Customers who are themselves financial institutions or MSBs requiring correspondent-style due diligence
