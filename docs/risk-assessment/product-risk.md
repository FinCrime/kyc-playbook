# Product / Service Risk

## Overview

Different financial products and services carry inherent ML/TF risk based on their structure, speed, anonymity potential, and cross-border reach.

## High-Risk Products/Services

| Product | Risk Factor |
|---|---|
| Correspondent banking | Indirect exposure to underlying respondent bank customers |
| Trade finance | High TBML exposure |
| Private banking | High-value, often complex, discretion expected |
| Wire transfers (especially international) | Speed, cross-border reach |
| Cryptocurrency products | Pseudonymity, speed |
| Cash management/cash-intensive products | Placement risk |
| Prepaid cards/e-wallets | Can be anonymous, easily transferable |

## Lower-Risk Products

- Standard retail deposit accounts with transaction limits
- Regulated investment products with strict KYC at point of sale
- Domestic-only payment products with transaction monitoring
