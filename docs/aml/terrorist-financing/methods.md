# Terrorist Financing Methods

## Overview

Terrorist organizations use a wide variety of methods to raise, move, and store funds. Understanding these methods is essential for identifying TF red flags during investigations.

## Fundraising Methods

### Charitable Sector Abuse
NPOs are exploited through:
- Diversion of donated funds
- Creation of fake charities
- Abuse of legitimate charities with poor governance

### State Sponsorship
Some states have historically funded terrorist groups as instruments of foreign policy. Funds may appear as trade credits, diplomatic transfers, or intelligence payments.

### Criminal Activities
Many terrorist groups are self-financing through:
- **Drug trafficking** — Taliban, FARC, Hezbollah
- **Kidnap for ransom** — Al-Qaeda affiliates, ISIS
- **Smuggling** — Oil, antiquities (ISIS), cigarettes
- **Cybercrime** — North Korea-linked Lazarus Group, ransomware

### Legitimate Business
Front businesses or investments generate income that funds operations while appearing legitimate.

## Value Transfer Methods

### Formal Financial System
Wire transfers, mobile money, banking — increasingly scrutinized but still used, especially for small amounts.

### Informal Value Transfer
**Hawala / Hundi** — An informal money transfer system based on trust networks. A sender deposits funds with a broker (hawaladar) in one country; the broker's counterpart pays the equivalent to a recipient in another country. No physical transfer of funds occurs. Settlement happens through reverse transactions, trade goods, or periodic cash reconciliation.

Red flags for hawala:
- No formal wire transfer records
- Cash deposits followed by instructions referencing codes
- Agent businesses (travel agents, exchange houses) in high-risk areas

### Cryptocurrency
Used for:
- Fundraising via cryptocurrency donation addresses (publicized on extremist forums)
- Moving funds across borders pseudonymously
- Converting between coins to obscure trails

### Prepaid Cards / E-Wallets
Small-denomination, anonymous prepaid products can be used to move funds in hard-to-trace ways.

### Trade-Based Value Transfer
Over/under-invoicing used to move value without currency transfer.

## Storage Methods

- Cash (hidden, buried)
- Precious metals and stones
- Real estate
- Cryptocurrency cold wallets
