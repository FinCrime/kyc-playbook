# Welcome to KYC Playbook 🎓

**KYC Playbook** is the world's most comprehensive free learning platform for Anti-Money Laundering (AML), Know Your Customer (KYC), Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD), and financial crime compliance.

Whether you're a student entering the compliance field, a career-switcher, a working analyst seeking to sharpen your skills, or preparing for the CAMS certification — this platform is built for you.

## What You'll Learn

This platform covers the complete spectrum of financial crime compliance:

| Domain | Topics Covered |
|---|---|
| **AML Fundamentals** | Money laundering stages, predicate crimes, terrorist financing, typologies |
| **KYC / CDD** | Customer identification, risk rating, simplified and enhanced due diligence |
| **EDD** | Investigation process, source of funds, source of wealth, narrative writing |
| **KYB** | Business verification, UBO identification, merchant risk assessment |
| **Sanctions** | OFAC, UN, EU, UK sanctions; 50% Rule; hit resolution |
| **PEP** | Categories, RCAs, investigation methodology |
| **Adverse Media** | Investigation techniques, risk assessment |
| **OSINT** | Google dorks, WHOIS, business registries, court records |
| **Transaction Monitoring** | Alert management, investigation, red flags |
| **SAR / STR** | Multi-jurisdiction filing, narrative writing |
| **Career** | Interview prep, CAMS study guide, certifications |

## How to Use This Platform

### For Beginners
Start with the [Learning Paths guide](/docs/introduction/learning-paths) to follow a structured curriculum. Begin with [AML Fundamentals](/docs/aml/money-laundering/overview) before advancing to investigation topics.

### For Working Analysts
Use the platform as a reference. The [Glossary](/docs/glossary), [Cheat Sheets](/docs/cheat-sheets/overview), and [Templates](/docs/templates/overview) are particularly useful for day-to-day work.

### For Interview Preparation
Go directly to [Career & Interview Prep](/docs/career/overview) for 500+ curated questions with model answers across all AML/KYC roles.

### For CAMS Candidates
Follow the [CAMS Preparation guide](/docs/career/certifications/cams) with structured study plan, practice questions, and flashcards.

### For Hands-On Practice
Visit [Practice Labs](/docs/labs/overview) to simulate real investigations — merchant risk, EDD, PEP screening, and SAR writing.

## About This Platform

KYC Playbook was created by AML/KYC practitioners who noticed the lack of high-quality, free, comprehensive educational resources for the compliance field.

Content is:
- ✅ Written by working practitioners
- ✅ Grounded in real investigative experience
- ✅ Continuously updated as regulations evolve
- ✅ 100% free, no paywall, no subscription
- ✅ Open source on GitHub — contributions welcome
