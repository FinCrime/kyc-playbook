# Business Verification

## Overview

Business verification confirms that a corporate entity legally exists, is properly registered, and is in good standing with relevant authorities. This is the foundational step before assessing ownership or risk.

## Verification Checklist

- [ ] Entity registered with appropriate company registry
- [ ] Registration number matches across all submitted documents
- [ ] Entity status is "active" / "in good standing" (not dissolved, struck off, or under liquidation)
- [ ] Registered address verified
- [ ] Business activity/SIC code consistent with stated business purpose
- [ ] Incorporation date consistent with claimed business history
- [ ] Required business licenses obtained (where applicable to the industry)

## Commercial Registries by Jurisdiction

| Jurisdiction | Registry |
|---|---|
| United Kingdom | Companies House |
| United States | State Secretary of State databases (varies by state); OpenCorporates aggregator |
| India | Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) — Registrar of Companies (ROC) |
| Singapore | ACRA (Accounting and Corporate Regulatory Authority) |
| UAE | Department of Economic Development (per Emirate); DIFC/ADGM for free zones |
| European Union | National registries; BRIS (Business Registers Interconnection System) |
| Hong Kong | Companies Registry |
| Australia | ASIC (Australian Securities & Investments Commission) |

## Red Flags in Business Verification

- Entity not found in expected registry
- Registration recently created with no operating history, paired with a request for high-value/high-risk services
- Registered address is a known mass-incorporation address (hundreds of unrelated companies)
- Business activity code inconsistent with stated business purpose
- Entity status shows "dissolved," "struck off," or "inactive"
- Frequent changes in registered address, directors, or business name
- Licensing requirements for the stated activity not met (e.g., financial services without a license)

## License Verification

For regulated industries, verify the specific license:

| Industry | License Type |
|---|---|
| Money Service Business | MSB registration (FinCEN, local regulator) |
| Payment institution | E-money/PI license |
| Gambling | Gaming license (jurisdiction-specific) |
| Securities/Investment | Broker-dealer or investment advisor registration |
| Insurance | Insurance license |
