# Merchant Investigation Process

## Step-by-Step Merchant Investigation

### Step 1: Application Review
- Review the merchant application form for completeness
- Confirm declared business type matches the application category requested

### Step 2: Entity Verification
- Verify company registration and good standing
- Identify UBOs and directors
- Screen all UBOs/directors against sanctions, PEP, and adverse media

### Step 3: Website/App Review
- Visit the live website/app
- Confirm functionality (can you add to cart, complete checkout flow in test mode?)
- Verify product descriptions, pricing, and terms align with the application
- Check for required legal pages: Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, Refund Policy, Contact Information
- Cross-check the business name/branding on the website against the registered entity name

### Step 4: Product/Service Risk Classification
- Confirm correct MCC classification
- Check against prohibited/restricted business lists
- For restricted categories, verify required licenses (e.g., gambling license, pharmacy license)

### Step 5: Beneficial Ownership & Control Screening
- Identify and screen all UBOs (apply standard UBO thresholds)
- Identify and screen all directors/signatories
- Check for any negative history (terminated merchant accounts, prior fraud)

### Step 6: Financial & Processing History Review
- For existing merchants switching providers: request prior processing statements
- Assess historical chargeback and refund rates
- Verify consistency between claimed transaction volumes and business size/capacity

### Step 7: Risk Scoring and Decision
- Apply merchant risk scoring model (entity + product + processing risk factors)
- Determine approval, conditional approval (with monitoring/reserve requirements), or decline

### Step 8: Ongoing Monitoring Setup
- Establish transaction monitoring thresholds
- Set chargeback/refund rate alert triggers
- Schedule periodic re-review (especially for higher-risk categories)

## Merchant Investigation Documentation Checklist

- [ ] Business registration documents verified
- [ ] UBO and director identification complete
- [ ] All relevant parties screened (sanctions/PEP/adverse media)
- [ ] Website/app functionality verified
- [ ] MCC classification confirmed and appropriate
- [ ] Required licenses verified (if applicable)
- [ ] Terms of service, privacy policy, refund policy present
- [ ] Processing history reviewed (if applicable)
- [ ] Risk score calculated and documented
- [ ] Decision rationale documented

## Ongoing Merchant Monitoring

Once onboarded, merchants require continuous monitoring:

| Metric | Alert Threshold (typical) |
|---|---|
| Chargeback ratio | >1% of transactions (varies by card scheme/category) |
| Refund ratio | Significant deviation from category norm |
| Transaction volume spike | >200% month-over-month growth without notice |
| Average ticket size change | Significant deviation from approved profile |
| Customer complaints | Pattern of complaints regarding non-delivery/misrepresentation |
