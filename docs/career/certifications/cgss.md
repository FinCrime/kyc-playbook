# CGSS — Certified Global Sanctions Specialist

## What Is CGSS?

**CGSS (Certified Global Sanctions Specialist)** is a specialized certification administered by ACAMS, focused specifically on sanctions compliance — a valuable credential for professionals whose roles concentrate heavily on sanctions screening, hit resolution, and sanctions program management.

## Typical Content Areas

- Major sanctions regimes (OFAC, UN, EU, UK, and other national frameworks)
- Sanctions list structures and designation types
- Ownership and control rules (including the 50% Rule concept)
- Sanctions program design and governance
- License applications and compliance exceptions

:::tip Verify Current Details
As with all certifications, confirm the current exam content outline, format, and eligibility requirements directly from ACAMS before planning your study approach.
:::

## Who Should Consider CGSS

This certification is particularly relevant for:
- Sanctions screening analysts and hit resolution specialists
- Compliance professionals at institutions with significant cross-border/correspondent banking exposure
- Professionals seeking to specialize within the broader AML/financial crime field
