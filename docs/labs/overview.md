# Practice Labs

## What Are Practice Labs?

Practice labs are hands-on, scenario-based exercises that simulate real-world AML/KYC investigations. Each lab provides a realistic scenario with evidence, objectives, investigation steps, hints, and a full model solution.

Learning theory is essential — but labs build the **applied judgment** that separates good analysts from great ones.

## How to Use the Labs

1. **Read the scenario** — Do not skip ahead to the solution
2. **Work through the investigation** — Try to identify red flags and next steps independently
3. **Use hints** sparingly — only if genuinely stuck
4. **Compare your findings** to the model solution
5. **Reflect on gaps** — What did you miss? What would you do differently?

## Why Labs Matter for Interviews

Most AML/KYC interviews include scenario-based questions that require applying knowledge, not just reciting definitions. Lab practice builds the mental frameworks to work through unfamiliar scenarios confidently.
