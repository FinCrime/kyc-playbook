# Lab: PEP Screening

## Scenario

Your screening tool returns the following match during onboarding of a new customer, **Mr. James Mensah Asante** (DOB: 15 June 1971, Ghanaian national):

> **MATCH RESULT**
> Name: James Mensah Asante
> DOB: 15 June 1971
> Nationality: Ghana
> PEP Category: Former Minister of Finance, Republic of Ghana
> Tenure: 2017–2021
> Match Score: 94%

---

## Your Objectives

1. Is this a true match or false positive? What additional steps confirm it?
2. What EDD is now required?
3. What specific documentation would you request regarding Source of Wealth?
4. What is the appropriate risk rating and review frequency?

---

## Model Solution

<details>
<summary>✅ Reveal Model Solution</summary>

### Step 1: Confirm True Match
- Name: Exact match ✅
- DOB: Exact match (15 June 1971) ✅
- Nationality: Matches ✅
- **Conclusion: True PEP match confirmed** — 94% score + all identifiers align; this is not a false positive

### Step 2: EDD Required
As a **Foreign PEP** (Ghana is a foreign jurisdiction from most institutions' perspectives) who served as **Minister of Finance** (a Tier 1 PEP role with direct control over financial flows and public funds), full EDD is mandatory:
- Enhanced identity verification
- Source of wealth investigation
- Adverse media deep dive (including Ghanaian-language sources)
- Senior management approval before onboarding

### Step 3: Source of Wealth Focus Areas
- Ministry of Finance salary history (public information, typically modest relative to declared HNW)
- Pre-public-office business interests (any business predating 2017?)
- Post-office business activities (since leaving office in 2021)
- Any disclosed investments, directorships, or advisory roles

**Specific red flags to probe:** Minister of Finance has direct authority over budgets, contracts, and banking regulation — highest-corruption-risk category of public office globally.

### Step 4: Risk Rating and Monitoring
- **Risk Rating: HIGH** (automatic for active/recent PEP, especially Tier 1 role)
- **Review Frequency: Annual minimum** (many institutions apply semi-annual for Tier 1 / former Finance Ministers)
- **PEP Designation:** Maintained for at least 12 months post-office (most institutions maintain indefinitely for Tier 1 roles from jurisdictions with elevated corruption risk)

</details>
